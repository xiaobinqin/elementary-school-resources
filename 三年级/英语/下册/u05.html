Unit 5 Do You Like Pears?

三年级 · 英语 · 下册
🏠 首页› 三年级› 英语› 下册› Unit 5 Do You Like Pears?
📚 本章知识点
知识点 1

水果蔬菜词汇：pear, apple, banana, orange, grape, tomato, carrot

知识点 2

喜好询问：Do you like...? Yes, I do./No, I don't.

知识点 3

购物用语：How many...do you want?

知识点 4

数量词：some, many, a lot of

⚡ 重点难点
  • 水果蔬菜的可数与不可数（some water / some apples）
  • How many vs How much（可数/不可数）
  • Do you like...? 句型的扩展
  • 健康饮食的表达
✏️ 巩固练习（8题）
💡 先独立思考作答，再点击查看答案！
1
翻译水果蔬菜：梨（　）香蕉（　）葡萄（　）胡萝卜（　）西红柿（　）
✅ 答案
pear; banana; grape; carrot; tomato
📝 解析：水果蔬菜词汇
2
Do you like pears? 用英语回答（喜欢）
✅ 答案
Yes, I do. I like pears very much.
📝 解析：水果喜好表达
3
翻译：你想要几个苹果？
✅ 答案
How many apples do you want?
📝 解析：询问数量句型
4
用some/many/a lot of造句（各一句）
✅ 答案
I have some apples. I have many friends. There are a lot of books in the library.
📝 解析：数量词使用
5
健康饮食：写出3种健康食物（英语）及简单说明
✅ 答案
apples (good for health), carrots (good for eyes), bananas (give energy)
📝 解析：健康食物介绍
6
完成购物对话：A: ___you like grapes? B: Yes, ___. A: How many ___? B: ___ kilograms, please.
✅ 答案
Do; I do; do you want/grapes; Two
📝 解析：购物对话填空
7
写出你最喜欢和最不喜欢的水果（英语各2种）
✅ 答案
开放性：I like apples and bananas. I don't like grapes and oranges.
📝 解析：个人喜好
8
翻译：多吃水果蔬菜对健康有好处。
✅ 答案
Eating more fruits and vegetables is good for your health.
📝 解析：健康建议句型
🚀 拔高训练（5题）
💡 这些题有一定难度，挑战一下自己吧！完成后再查看答案。
1
写一段关于你最喜欢的水果的英语短文（5句话） ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
My favourite fruit is the apple. It is red and delicious. Apples are very healthy. I eat one apple every day. An apple a day keeps the doctor away!
📝 解析：水果介绍短文
2
设计健康食谱（英语）：写出一天的早中晚餐，都包含水果或蔬菜 ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
Breakfast: porridge with bananas; Lunch: rice with carrots and tomatoes; Dinner: noodles with green vegetables.（开放性）
📝 解析：健康食谱设计
3
创作：用英语写一首关于水果的小诗（4行） ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
Red apples, yellow bananas, orange grapes on the vine. Eat your fruits every day, and you will feel just fine!（开放性）
📝 解析：英语诗歌创作
4
调查：问3个同学最喜欢的水果，用英语写出调查结果 ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
开放性，格式：[name] likes [fruit]. [name] likes [fruit]. [name] likes [fruit]. The most popular fruit is [fruit].
📝 解析：英语调查报告
5
【综合题】扮演超市收银员：用英语写出完整的购物对话（8句），包含水果蔬菜 ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
Cashier: Good morning! Can I help you? Customer: Yes, I'd like some apples and bananas. Cashier: How many apples? Customer: Six, please. Cashier: And how many bananas? Customer: Three. Cashier: Anything else? Customer: No, thank you. How much is it? Cashier: It's 20 yuan. Customer: Here you are. Cashier: Thank you, goodbye!
📝 解析：综合购物对话
