Unit 4 My Home

四年级 · 英语 · 上册
🏠 首页› 四年级› 英语› 上册› Unit 4 My Home
📚 本章知识点
知识点 1

房间词汇：living room, bedroom, bathroom, kitchen, study

知识点 2

家具词汇：sofa, fridge, washing machine, telephone, bed

知识点 3

描述家的位置：on the first floor, upstairs, downstairs

知识点 4

询问和描述：What room is this? It's the...

⚡ 重点难点
  • 房间和家具词汇的记忆
  • 楼层的英语表达（first floor=一楼）
  • 描述家庭环境的完整句子
  • 数量词结合家具的描述
✏️ 巩固练习（8题）
💡 先独立思考作答，再点击查看答案！
1
翻译房间：客厅（　）卧室（　）厨房（　）书房（　）浴室（　）
✅ 答案
living room; bedroom; kitchen; study; bathroom
📝 解析：房间词汇
2
翻译家具：冰箱（　）洗衣机（　）沙发（　）电话（　）
✅ 答案
fridge; washing machine; sofa; telephone
📝 解析：家具词汇
3
翻译：厨房在一楼，卧室在二楼。
✅ 答案
The kitchen is on the first floor. The bedroom is on the second floor.
📝 解析：楼层表达
4
描述你家里的一个房间（英语3句话）
✅ 答案
My bedroom is on the second floor. There is a bed and a desk. My room is cosy and tidy.（开放性）
📝 解析：房间描述
5
What room is this? （指厨房）用英语回答
✅ 答案
It's the kitchen.
📝 解析：识别房间
6
写出你家里有哪些家具（英语5种）
✅ 答案
sofa, fridge, bed, desk, washing machine等
📝 解析：家具词汇
7
造句：We cook in the ___, we sleep in the ___.
✅ 答案
We cook in the kitchen. We sleep in the bedroom.
📝 解析：房间功能描述
8
翻译：我们家有三间卧室和两间浴室。
✅ 答案
We have three bedrooms and two bathrooms.
📝 解析：描述家的数量
🚀 拔高训练（5题）
💡 这些题有一定难度，挑战一下自己吧！完成后再查看答案。
1
写英语描述你的家（8句话），包括房间数量、位置和家具 ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
My home has three bedrooms. The living room is on the first floor. There is a big sofa in the living room. The kitchen has a fridge and a stove. My bedroom is upstairs. There is a desk next to my bed. My home is warm and comfortable. I love my home.
📝 解析：家庭综合描述
2
设计理想住宅：用英语描述你梦想中的家（6句话） ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
My dream home is a big house in the countryside. It has five bedrooms and three bathrooms. There is a swimming pool in the garden. The kitchen is modern and bright. Every room has big windows. I would love to live there!（开放性）
📝 解析：理想住宅描述
3
写英语信：邀请朋友来你家做客（6句话） ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
Dear [name], Please come to my home on Saturday! My home is at [address]. Turn left at the school and go straight. My home is the third house on the right. I will cook delicious food! See you then! [name]
📝 解析：邀请信写作
4
比较：公寓和独栋别墅，各有什么优缺点？（英语4句话） ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
An apartment is small but easy to manage. A house is big and has a garden. Apartments are usually cheaper. Houses give more space and privacy.
📝 解析：居住类型比较
5
【综合题】角色扮演：带外国朋友参观你家，用英语介绍每个房间（10句话） ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
Welcome to my home! This is the living room. We have a big sofa and a TV. Here is the kitchen. We cook and eat here. Upstairs are the bedrooms. This is my bedroom. I sleep and study here. The bathroom is next to my room. I hope you like my home!
📝 解析：参观介绍对话
