Unit 5 What Would You Like?

四年级 · 英语 · 上册
🏠 首页› 四年级› 英语› 上册› Unit 5 What Would You Like?
📚 本章知识点
知识点 1

餐食词汇：noodles, dumplings, porridge, steak, salad

知识点 2

点餐表达：What would you like for...? I'd like...

知识点 3

用餐礼貌语：Please have some... / No, thanks, I'm full.

知识点 4

描述食物：delicious, yummy, sour, sweet, spicy

⚡ 重点难点
  • Would like和like的区别（Would like=想要）
  • 餐食词汇（特别是中式食物的英语）
  • 味道形容词：sour, sweet, spicy, bitter
  • 在餐厅场景中流畅对话
✏️ 巩固练习（8题）
💡 先独立思考作答，再点击查看答案！
1
翻译餐食：面条（　）饺子（　）粥（　）牛排（　）沙拉（　）
✅ 答案
noodles; dumplings; porridge; steak; salad
📝 解析：餐食词汇
2
区别：I like noodles 和 I would like noodles（什么区别？）
✅ 答案
I like noodles（我喜欢面条，表示一般习惯）；I would like noodles（我想要面条，表示此刻的需求，通常用于点餐）
📝 解析：like vs would like
3
What would you like for lunch? 用英语回答
✅ 答案
I'd like (some) noodles/dumplings, please.
📝 解析：点餐句型
4
翻译味道：甜的（　）酸的（　）辣的（　）苦的（　）
✅ 答案
sweet; sour; spicy; bitter
📝 解析：味道形容词
5
用英语描述你最喜欢的食物的味道（2句话）
✅ 答案
I love dumplings. They are delicious and not too spicy.（开放性）
📝 解析：食物味道描述
6
No, thanks, I'm full. 这句话在什么情况下说？
✅ 答案
当别人邀请你吃东西但你已经吃饱了的时候，礼貌地拒绝
📝 解析：礼貌拒绝的场景
7
写出一个完整的点餐对话（英语4句）
✅ 答案
A: What would you like? B: I'd like noodles, please. A: Would you like something to drink? B: Yes, juice please. Thank you!
📝 解析：点餐对话
8
翻译：中国食物非常美味，我最喜欢饺子。
✅ 答案
Chinese food is very delicious. My favourite is dumplings.
📝 解析：中国食物介绍
🚀 拔高训练（5题）
💡 这些题有一定难度，挑战一下自己吧！完成后再查看答案。
1
在餐厅写一段完整的点餐英语对话（10句话），包含点菜、询问味道、结账 ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
Waiter: Good evening! May I take your order? Customer: Yes, I'd like the noodles. Waiter: What would you like to drink? Customer: Orange juice, please. Waiter: Anything else? Customer: Are the dumplings spicy? Waiter: They're a little spicy. Customer: OK, I'll have some. Waiter: Your total is 50 yuan. Customer: Here you are. Thank you!
📝 解析：完整餐厅对话
2
写英语食谱介绍：介绍一道中国菜的做法（5句话） ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
Dumplings are a popular Chinese food. First, make the dough with flour. Then, fill it with meat and vegetables. Next, fold and seal the edges. Finally, cook in boiling water. They are delicious!
📝 解析：食谱介绍（步骤描述）
3
比较中西方用餐礼仪（各写2点英语） ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
Chinese: use chopsticks, it's polite to serve others food; Western: use a knife and fork, eat from your own plate, say 'please' and 'thank you'
📝 解析：用餐文化比较
4
创作：设计一份有创意的菜单（英语），中西结合 ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
开放性：早餐：Chinese porridge with bread; 午餐：Noodles or salad; 晚餐：Dumplings or steak
📝 解析：菜单设计
5
【综合题】为家庭聚会写英语邀请函，包括日期、地点和餐食（8句话） ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
Dear friends and family, Please join us for a family dinner! It will be on Saturday, 3 March. The dinner is at our home. We will have dumplings, noodles and much more. There will also be fruit and dessert. Please come at 6 pm. We look forward to seeing you! Love, [name]
📝 解析：综合邀请函写作
