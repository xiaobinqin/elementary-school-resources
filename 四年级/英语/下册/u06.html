Unit 6 Shopping

📚 本章知识点
知识点 1

购物词汇：shop, supermarket, market, sale, price, receipt

知识点 2

价格表达：How much is/are...? It costs...yuan.

知识点 3

购物决策：I'll take it./I'll buy it./That's too expensive.

知识点 4

比较价格：cheaper, more expensive

⚡ 重点难点
  • 价格的英语表达（元/角）
  • I'll take it / I'll buy it的使用场景
  • 比较价格的形容词（比较级）
  • 购物全程的流畅英语对话
✏️ 巩固练习（8题）
💡 先独立思考作答，再点击查看答案！
1
翻译购物词汇：商店（　）超市（　）市场（　）价格（　）收据（　）
✅ 答案
shop; supermarket; market; price; receipt
📝 解析：购物词汇
2
How much is this shirt? （50元）用英语回答
✅ 答案
It costs 50 yuan. / It's 50 yuan.
📝 解析：价格询问回答
3
翻译：这件衬衫太贵了，我不买了。
✅ 答案
This shirt is too expensive. I won't buy it.
📝 解析：表达嫌贵
4
用英语表达：这个更便宜，我要这个。
✅ 答案
This one is cheaper. I'll take this one.
📝 解析：比较和决策
5
写出一个购物场景的4句英语对话
✅ 答案
A: How much is this? B: It's 30 yuan. A: That's a bit expensive. Can I have a discount? B: OK, 25 yuan!（开放性）
📝 解析：购物对话
6
cheaper vs more expensive：苹果5元，草莓10元，哪个更便宜？（英语表达）
✅ 答案
Apples are cheaper. Strawberries are more expensive.
📝 解析：比较价格
7
翻译：打折啦！所有商品五折优惠！
✅ 答案
Sale! Everything is 50% off!
📝 解析：打折英语表达
8
写出你购物时最常说的3句英语
✅ 答案
How much is this? I'll take it. Do you have this in blue?（开放性）
📝 解析：购物常用语
🚀 拔高训练（5题）
💡 这些题有一定难度，挑战一下自己吧！完成后再查看答案。
1
写完整的购物对话（英语10句话）：在服装店买一件礼物 ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
Shop assistant: Good afternoon! Can I help you? Customer: Yes, I'm looking for a birthday present. Assistant: How about this jacket? Customer: It looks nice. How much is it? Assistant: It's 120 yuan. Customer: That's a little expensive. Do you have a discount? Assistant: Today we have 20% off. Customer: So it's 96 yuan? Assistant: Yes, that's right. Customer: Great, I'll take it! Here's 100 yuan. Assistant: Here's your change and receipt. Thank you!
📝 解析：完整购物对话
2
写购物英语日记（6句话）：去超市买东西 ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
Yesterday I went to the supermarket with mum. We bought vegetables, fruit and milk. I helped choose the apples. Each kilogram was 8 yuan. The total was 50 yuan. I paid the cashier and got the receipt.
📝 解析：购物日记
3
设计商店广告（英语4句话）：吸引顾客 ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
Welcome to [name] Shop! We have the freshest fruits and vegetables. All items are at low prices. Come and get great value today!（开放性）
📝 解析：广告设计
4
比较：网购和实体店购物各有什么优缺点？（英语4句话） ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
Online shopping is convenient and often cheaper. But you can't try the product before buying. Shopping in a store is fun and you can see the items. But it takes more time.
📝 解析：购物方式比较
5
【综合题/期末】写一篇英语作文：四年级我最大的进步（英语10句话），回顾学习成果 ⭐⭐⭐
✅ 答案
开放性，包含：英语学习进步、最喜欢的单元、对五年级的期望
📝 解析：期末综合写作
